• The first row of the CSV file must be the column titles
  • Column titles are all lower-case, separated by underscores (no spaces).
  • Accepted column titles are as follows:
    • last_name, first_name (if "Last, First" are in one column) OR
    • first_name (First Name in its own column)
    • last_name (Last Name in its own column)
    • email
    • address
    • address_city
    • address_state
    • address_zipcode
    • mailing_address
    • mailing_address2 (optional)
    • mailing_city
    • mailing_state
    • mailing_zipcode
